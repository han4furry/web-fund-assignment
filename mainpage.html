What is Living a Leafier Life?

Living a Leafier life aims to provide suggestions on plant-based substitutes to some of the most commonly used animal-based ingredients when cooking.

★ Taking the First Step

If you have found yourself here, there may be a variety of reasons as to why. Perhaps you actually are making an attempt to live a 'leafier life', but there is of course the possibility that you are allergic or intolerant towards an ingredient that you now need, or the very small chance that you simply do not have a required ingredient but somehow own plant based ingredients that you could use as a substitute.

Regardless of which category you fit into, we welcome you!

★ Tried and Tested

We have done our best to ensure that with every substitute suggestion, a review will be included so that you may decide which substitute works best for you.
